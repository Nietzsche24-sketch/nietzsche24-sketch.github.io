NY-ADHD Questionnaire

A simple reflective questionnaire for adult ADHD-related patterns. This tool is offered as a personal resource and not as diagnosis or treatment.

Before you begin

This questionnaire is provided for thoughtful self-reflection and discussion. It is not a clinic intake form, not a diagnosis, and not medical advice.

Choose the response that best reflects how often each statement applies.

Adult ADHD Reflection Questions

1. I have difficulty staying focused on tasks that require sustained attention.

2. I begin things with good intentions but often leave them unfinished.

3. I am easily distracted by things going on around me.

4. I have trouble organizing tasks, papers, or daily responsibilities.

5. I put off tasks even when I know they are important.

6. I lose things I need for everyday work or home life.

7. I feel restless, inwardly driven, or unable to settle easily.

8. I interrupt, speak too quickly, or answer before others are finished.

9. My time management is often poorer than I expect it to be.

10. These patterns have affected work, relationships, or daily life over time.
