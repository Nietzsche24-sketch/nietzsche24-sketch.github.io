Life Timeline

A life told through seasons and milestones

A simple timeline of family roots, work, church life, and enduring interests.

Early Years

Beginnings

Born into a family shaped by faith, steadiness, modesty, and close community life. The earliest years were marked by home, church, and a quiet sense of duty.

School Years

Character taking shape

Quiet, observant, and thoughtful from an early age, these were years of learning, helping, and noticing the kinds of details others often passed by.

Young Adulthood

Work, responsibility, and calling

Adulthood brought wider responsibilities: work, service, faith community, and the beginnings of long-standing interests that would deepen over time.

Family Life

Marriage and household

Marriage and family life became the center of daily rhythm. Much of what mattered most was built quietly through routine, care, and long faithfulness.

Later Work

Reliability and wisdom

These years were marked by practical judgment, dependable effort, and a reputation for careful listening before speaking.

Adult ADHD Interest

A thoughtful long-term concern

Over time, increasing attention was given to adult ADHD: not as a public identity, but as a practical field of observation, reflection, and useful tools for others.

Later Years

Memory, family, and legacy

Family widened into new generations. The pace softened, but the meaning of daily life grew clearer through church, family, stories, and small acts of care.

Today

A life worth preserving well

This website gathers photographs, recorded reflections, family notes, and resource materials in one place so that what is quiet and meaningful is not easily lost.
